FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS -



How is Codespace different from a other development fests?

Codespace is you ideal innovation incubator. A Hackathon that embraces designers, developers, entrepreneurs, thinkers, doers and anyone who has the potential to create something brilliant.

How much do I need to pay to register? Do I pay in cash or credit?

We have kept a nominal fee of INR100. During 24hr Hackathon we will provide you with WiFi, food, goodies and a lot more.

What is the maximum number of people allowed in a team?

A maximum of 5 people per team. Don't have team-mates? We got you covered! We provide you with chat rooms on slack. Details to join are mentioned in the above section. Just hit Register!

How do I know I have been short-listed for the hack?

Register your project idea on this website. We'll review it and send you a mail with your Hack Ticket. Please bring a copy of this ticket with you when you come for the hack.

What if my partner was waitlisted but I wasn’t?

The selected teams are those who have passed the filtered process. However, there is a slight chance of prioritizing individual talent. So give the registration form your best. For such cases, do contact us.

What if I can’t attend after all?

If your spot has been confirmed by us but you can no longer attend, please contact us right away so that we can give your spot to someone who’s waitlisted. We would appreciate your efforts!

How do I check in upon arrival?

The selected members are sent their respective DevTickets, which has a unique code for each individual Please bring this ticket to the fest and you are good! You can also bring your favourite pillow but don't forget the ticket.
